Career

Artist Intro

Ricky Gervais is an English comedian, actor, writer, producer, director and apparently musician. He established small time celebrity status locally in Great Britain through the 90’s and achieved greater mainstream fame in 2001 with his BBC TV mock documentary The Office.

Gervais initially worked in the music industry, chasing a career as a pop star in the 80’s before turning to comedy. He is a well-established stand-up comedian, writer, producer and director.

Ricky Gervais has won a plethora of awards. Here are some of his most notable:
  • 7 BAFTA Awards
  • 5 British Comedy Awards
  • 2 Emmy Awards
  • 3 Golden Globe Awards
  • 2 Rose d'Or Awards
  • Whitley, Reading, UK (1961 – 1980)
  • London, UK (1980 – mid 1990’s)
  • Hampstead, UK & New York City, USA (current)
